Interests

Pengcheng's interests include deep learning, natural language processing, centralized/distributed RDBMS internals, and Cloud computing, etc. He has over 50 papers published or accepted by international conferences and journals (e.g., SIGMOD, SOCC, VLDB and ICDE) with over 1500 citations. He is a recipient of multiple best paper awards. He holds a plethora of US and China patents. He is an Apache Hive PMC member and Apache ORC committer. He is a senior member of IEEE and a member of ACM.

More information can be found in Google Scholar, DBLP, LinkedIn.

Experiences

  • 2017 - present: Tech lead manager, Amazon
  • 2014 - 2017: Apache Hive PMC member, committer, release manager, Cloudera
  • 2012 - 2014: Researcher, NEC Research Labs America
  • 2012: PhD in Computer Science, Georgia Tech
  • 2008: ME, PhD in Automation, Tsinghua University
